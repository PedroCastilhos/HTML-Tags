**Formatting elements were designed to display special types of text:**

1. `<b>` - Bold text
2. `<strong>` - Important text
3. `<i>` - Italic text
4. `<em>` - Emphasized text
5. `<mark>` - Marked text
6. `<small>` - Smaller text
7. `<del>` - Deleted text
8. `<ins>` - Inserted text
9. `<sub>` - Subscript text
10. `<sup>` - Superscript text
